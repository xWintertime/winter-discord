About

Winter's Discord is a community made discord filled with several fun and engaging bots, enjoyable content, and ultimately to bridge the gap between the writer and reader for an entertaining and exciting experience. If you are interested in any of Winter's web novels, or wish to be featured with your own. This discord is the place to share your ideas!

The discord has several features such as:

  • Patreon Integration
  • Unique and Fun Roles
  • Bots and Music Bots in General
  • Streaming Alerts
  • RPG Systems and Levels
  • Custom commands
  • Community Featured Novels
  • And much, much more!

Rules

We have a growing community with a focus on web novels.  Things such as Web novel announcements, updates, and features will be included in this discord, along with special roles, a complete and comprehensive discord setup, and reader feedback. We will also implement other author works in our discord in the future!

Here are some of the basic rules of our server:

  • This Server is PG-13! Please keep swearing to a minimum
  • No Racial or Religious Slurs or anything of the sort.
  • No Spamming the discord!
  • Please listen to moderators!
  • Zero tolerance on innapropriate links.
  • Please use English as the primary language.
  • Enjoy yourselves!
